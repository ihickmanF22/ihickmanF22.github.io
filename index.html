Description

This game is inspired by the classic 'Space Invaders' where instead of controlling a ship moving through space, you control a character named Codey. As you progress throughout the game to complete three levels, you fly up along a vast landscape of clouds until you reach the sky's limit. The objective of the game, much like its 1978 predecessor, is to destroy all of the enemies. Alternatively, you can also win by surviving long enough. A more detailed description can be found under the Project Overview section in the game's comments.
